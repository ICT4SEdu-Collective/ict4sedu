# 🌎 Networks

A list of broader networks and coalitions advancing sustainable digitalization.

---

- **[Nationale Koalition voor Duurzame Digitalisering (NCDD)](https://coalitieduurzamedigitalisering.nl/)** – Dutch national coalition for sustainable digitalization, connecting organizations working toward greener digital transformation.
- **[Green Software Foundation](https://greensoftware.foundation/)** – Industry network developing standards, tools, and best practices for green software. (See also: [Tools](Tools.md))
- **[ICT4S Education Initiative](https://ict4sedu.wordpress.com)** – The network behind this repository, supporting educators and students in integrating sustainability into computing education.
