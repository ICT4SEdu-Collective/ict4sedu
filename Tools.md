# 🛠️ Tools, Frameworks and Guidelines

A collection of tools, frameworks, and guidelines for integrating sustainability into digital products, services, and processes — for developers, architects, requirements engineers, and decision-makers alike.

---

## 🧰 Tools for Measuring CO₂ Emissions & Environmental Impact

- **[GlobeMallow](https://globemallow.io)** – CO₂ calculator for websites.
- **[Green Web Foundation](https://www.thegreenwebfoundation.org/)** – Check whether websites run on green hosting; includes a JavaScript library for emissions measurement in web apps.
- **[Cleaner Web](https://cleaner-web.com/)** – Quick emissions analysis for web pages.
- **[Globalance World](https://fe.globalanceworld.com)** – Visualization of climate and financial data.

---

## 🧠 Frameworks & Guidelines for Sustainable Design

- **[Sustainability Awareness Framework (SusAF)](https://www.suso.academy/en/sustainability-awareness-framework-susaf/)** – Integrating sustainability into requirements engineering processes.
- **[SAF Toolkit](https://github.com/S2-group/SAF-Toolkit)** – Sustainability focus for software architecture and quality assurance.
- **[W3C Web Sustainability Guidelines (WSG)](https://www.w3.org/blog/2023/introducing-web-sustainability-guidelines/)** – Community draft for sustainable web design; technical recommendations for web projects.
- **[Green Software Foundation](https://greensoftware.foundation/)** – Industry standards, open-source tools, and educational resources.
- **[Green Software Foundation – Pattern Library](https://patterns.greensoftware.foundation/)** – Database of green software patterns for reducing CO₂ through software design.
- **[Digital Sustainability Center](https://digitalsustainabilitycenter.nl/)** – Platform for research and practice in digital sustainability.
- **[Blauer Engel for Software](https://www.blauer-engel.de/de/produktwelt/software)** – German eco-label (DE-UZ 215) for resource- and energy-efficient software.

## 📚 Guidelines & Standards for Sustainable Software Development

- **[Bitkom Guide Vol. 1](https://www.bitkom.org/sites/main/files/2024-12/publikation-ressourceneffizienz-im-software-lifecycle-teil1.pdf)** – *A roadmap for resource-efficient digital transformation.* Focus: Overview & orientation. Publisher: Bitkom.
- **[Bitkom Guide Vol. 2](https://www.bitkom.org/sites/main/files/2024-12/publikation-ressourceneffizienz-im-software-lifecycle-teil2.pdf)** – *Requirements clarification & conceptual work.* Focus: Anchoring sustainability early. Publisher: Bitkom.
- **[Bitkom Guide Vol. 3](https://www.bitkom.org/sites/main/files/2025-02/bitkom-leitfaden-ressourceneffizienz-im-software-lifecycle-band-3.pdf)** – *Development & operations.* Focus: Efficiency in coding, deployment & operations. Publisher: Bitkom.
