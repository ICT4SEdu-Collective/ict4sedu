# 👥 Communities

A list of active communities working at the intersection of ICT and sustainability.

---

- **[IREB SIG Sustainability (SIGsus)](https://ireb.org/en/community/special-interest-group/sig-sustainability)** – Special Interest Group within IREB focused on sustainability in requirements engineering.
- **[SUX Network](https://sustainableuxnetwork.com/)** – Community for sustainable UX design practitioners.
- **[ICT4S – ICT for Sustainability](https://conf.researchr.org/series/ict4s)** – Research community and conference series on ICT for sustainability.
- **[ICSE Track "Software Engineering in Society"](https://conf.researchr.org/track/icse-2025/icse-2025-software-engineering-in-society)** – Track at the International Conference on Software Engineering addressing societal impact.
- **[LIMITS](https://computingwithinlimits.org/2026/)** - Computing within LIMITS research community
